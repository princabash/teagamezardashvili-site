My philosophy

Prevention is measured in decades, not single readings. Most people are told their labs are “normal.” The more useful question — for a longer, healthier life — is whether they're optimal for that person's risk. CardioIQ operationalizes established cardiovascular-prevention frameworks — guideline-based risk thinking, ApoB- and Lp(a)-aware lipid assessment, and residual-risk logic — into one clear, evidence-anchored read. It doesn't invent its own rules; it makes the ones I use in clinic legible.

Important. CardioIQ provides educational interpretation of lab values against prevention-focused targets. It does not diagnose disease, recommend or prescribe treatment, promise outcomes, or replace your physician, who makes all clinical decisions. It is not a regulatory-cleared medical device.
